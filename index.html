About the project

The project seeks to solve difficulcy in accessing electric meters for inserting tokens after making payments to Tanesco, showing real-time consumption by tenants, clients, entreprenuers and consequently making it difficult to minimize wasteful routines as well as solving issues with power wastage and theft. We give solutions to all these problems with direct insertion, real time tracking and access via mobile phone and web portals. It is easy and a real saver.
